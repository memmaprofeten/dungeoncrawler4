21  * neighbouring rooms. A room can have a maximum of 4 neighbours (one per side)
22  * and these are (ideally) accessed when the player walks out of bounds in
23  * either of these four directions.
24  * The container also holds a pointer to the Room object itself, if the room is

26  * when the room itself is not loaded.
27  */
